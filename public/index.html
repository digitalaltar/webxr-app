Welcome to first.sequence, an immersive 3D experience showcasing the integration of human creative vision with AI and XR technology.

Each element of this project has been designed, coded, generated, and directed by me - Digital Altar. Careful attention has been put into the fusion of AI-generated music and 3D environments, powered by three.js, to create a cohesive and engaging digital experience.

The 3D experience presented here is available in any modern browser and compatible with many devices, including mobile phones and VR environments.
